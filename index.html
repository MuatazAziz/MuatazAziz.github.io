About

MY life before RE:CODED

In 2016, I had just graduated from Mosul University with a degree in architecture. I liked to develop my skills in technology and learn new skills. I heard about Re:Coded as a training bootcamp for displaced youth, making them software developers and providing them with jobs. I liked the idea so much and joined the program.

Within RE:CODED

Re:Coded was the perfect place for me to learn programming and develop other skills. With Re:Coded, I learned Ruby, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and many other tools, and I improved my English proficiency. I have met wonderful colleagues, mentors, teachers and staff. I learned a lot, and it has been a great experience.

After RE:CODED

I look forward to finding a good job opportunity where I can apply my software and design skills, and I will continue to develop my software capabilities. I also plan to work toward starting my own technology-focused business, and I hope to contribute to the community through software development.
